Being the first ever tech Community of Jammu and Kashmir, Sudan's
Technocrats Foundation or Sudan's Tech works for the students and by
the students.
Sudan's Tech is built for the betterment of underprivileged students
while providing them with better technical exposure for a great career
path to achieve their dreams.

VISION

Our vision is to create a better life for students through technology. Our aim
is to fill the gap of technical exposure and make students become industry-
ready. It is a community for students who desire to learn and work on
themselves. Technology inspires us to make this world a happy place for
students.
